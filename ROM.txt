Interrupts do different things depending on contents of the A register:

 A | BEHAVIOR
---+----------------------------------------------------------------------------
 0 | Copy the boot sector of the installed disk to memory and execute it. 
 1 | Flash ROM. B is offset to source
---+----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Interrupt 1 occurs at boot.
